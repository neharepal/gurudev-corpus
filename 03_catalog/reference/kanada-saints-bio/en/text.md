                          Chapter 1: INTRODUCTION


An a empt is made to present the Bio‐informa on of Kannada poet‐saints men oned by
Gurudeo R.D.Ranade in his ‘Pathway to God in Kannada literature’ and its source book
‘Kannada Paramartha Sopana’ and ‘Introduc on to Karnatak Mys cism’ with an objec ve of
making the reading of these works more interes ng and comprehensive. It is to be noted
that no such eﬀorts have been made earlier to present the bio‐informa on of Kannada poet
‐saints in English. English being an interna onal link language the informa on, I hope, will
reach a larger readership making it more useful for the students of Gurudeo’s literature.
Kannada poet‐saints have regarded their pen‐names as more signiﬁcant than their names
and have iden ﬁed their personality with those of their spiritual masters or their favourite
dei es. It therefore became an extremely diﬃcult task to determine the exact authorship of
the poems especially of those who are not much known to fame. It is also very diﬃcult to
pinpoint the loca on of places where they belonged as there exist two or more places with
the same name and situated geographically apart. Reorganiza on of states and forma on of
new districts over a period make the task more complicated. In the absence of proper his‐
torical records and diﬀerent stands taken by experts regarding names, places, period and
other personal informa on about the Kannada poet‐saints, it is not possible to assert that
the resumes presented here are exhaus ve in every respect and are totally free from errors.
This leaves ample scope for further work in this area for the researchers.
I have made an eﬀort to present the informa on in the form of popular photos/pictures and
maps based on a few books and Google search (Secondary Data) and ﬁeldwork conducted
by me and the others (Primary Data). I will feel amply rewarded if my work is taken forward
at least a few steps in the right direc on and inspires further research on the subject.
