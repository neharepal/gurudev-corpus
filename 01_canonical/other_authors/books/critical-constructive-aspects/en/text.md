Constructive Aspects of Professor R. D. Ranade’s
Philosophy and it will at once appeal to all keen
students of philosophy in general and to the Sadhakas

     The problem of supreme importance              to mankind,
according to    Prof. R. D. Ranade,       is    the problem of
the existence   of God    and    His relation       to man   and
nature.   Unquestionably the search after God            remains
the highest problem even        today   and     a   philosophical
justification for our spiritual life is as necessary today
as it was hundreds of years ago. Prof Ranade             devoted
the whole of his career to the solution of this problem
both from the standpoint of philosophy, i.e. the intelle-
ctual side and from      the standpoint of religion, i.e.
the practical   side.    He   was   led   to make a       living
acquaintance    with    mysticism and         religious thought
before he came to undertake a deep study of Indian
and Western philosophy.         Even after sucha study he
firmly believed that to come to grips          with the attain-

which the    Academy        must    institute   research.     Under
the head of European            Philosophy   the   Academy        must
take into account the development of the whole of Greek
Philosophy, as well as of Modern Philosophy and of
Contemporary Thought. In addition to research in
the history of Philosophy in general, the Academy must
devote its attention to Moral Philosophy, to Aesthetics,
to    Psychology    in    its   various   branches and       to      the
Philosophy of Science. Under the head of Indian
Philosophy it must consider the development of Indian
Philosophy from the times of the Vedas                 downwards

Mysticism in the         medieval    period, to even        contem-
porary tendencies of Indian thought.       Under the
heading of the Philosophy of Religion, it must consider
the history and the theology of all the different Faiths,
viz., Judaism, Christianity, Mohammedanism, Zoroa-
strianism, as well as Hinduism in its various branches

PROFESSOR B. R. KULKARNI, M.A.,LL.B., had the rare

good fortune of studying Philosophy under Shri Gurudev

R. D. Ranade in his Ashram at Nimbal and in Allahabad

University.   Had   also   the   proud   privilege   of   being

associated with monumental works of Shri Gurudev. On

the basis of the notes of Shri Gurudev, he prepared in

collaboration with Dr. V. H. Date, the book, " Vedant

as the Culmination of Indian Thought ".



Taught Philosophy in the R.       P. D. College,     Belgaum.

Later he worked as Professor of Philosophy at Yavatmal
and Bombay. He was Director of Bharatiya Vidya Bhavan,

New Delhi, there he delivered the lectures.
