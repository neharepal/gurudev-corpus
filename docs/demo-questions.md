# Demo Questions

Curated questions that produce strong, well-grounded answers — for demos and
as informal quality anchors. Add new ones as they prove themselves.

- What did Gurudev write about Kabir?

- Who was Nimbargi Maharaj and what is the Inchgeri sampradaya?
