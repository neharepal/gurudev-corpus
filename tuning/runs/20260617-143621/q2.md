## LLM answer

The corpus contains one notable reference to Gurudev's 60th year of age. In an athvani recounting an early event (before 1914), when Gurudev's mother Parvatabai went to Inchgeri and tearfully described her only son's serious brain illness to Shri Bhausaheb Maharaj, Maharaj looked towards the divine form before him and compassionately reassured her: "Up to sixty years, young Gurudev has no fear of death, and God intends to accomplish much great spiritual work through him — there is no need to worry even slightly about his health." This blessing gave both mother and son great relief, and it strengthened their faith and devotion toward the Sadguru. The mention of sixty years thus appears in the context of a divine assurance about Gurudev's longevity and spiritual mission, given by Bhausaheb Maharaj at a time when Gurudev was gravely ill in his early years.
