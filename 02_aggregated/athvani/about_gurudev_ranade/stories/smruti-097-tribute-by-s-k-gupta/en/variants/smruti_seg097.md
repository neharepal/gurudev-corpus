96- Prof. S. K. Gupta

Professor of Philosophy, M. P. C. College, Baripada (Orissa)

“A sketch of a God-realiser by one who is not so, is hardly adequate. Unless the spiritual depth which Ranade attained is fathomed, one can hardly depict him appropriately. Before I attempt a description of Ranade from the standpoint of a non-realiser of God, I bow down in veneration to the ‘Mysterium Tremendum’ that is Ranade.

His success as a teacher was manifest all the more at the level of guiding research. It was a privilege to be a research scholar under his guidance. Original ideas and scholarly dissertations are of the research students. First rate theses and books by research scholars are the outcome of his guidance.

It was not only his scholarship but also his unassuming nature that made Ranade an attractive personality. His affable nature and sparkling humour made his company enjoyable. All the books written by Ranade are outstanding, have an amazing depth and a wonderful charm. They bear the stamp of his deep spiritual insight. One can feel the influence of that liberated soul, through his books. It can lead a person to immortality.”117
