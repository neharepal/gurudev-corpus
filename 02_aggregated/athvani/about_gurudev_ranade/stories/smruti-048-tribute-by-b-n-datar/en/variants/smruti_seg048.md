47- Shri B. N. Datar

Minister of State in the Home Ministry

“Prof. Rambhau Ranade has been a spiritual guide or Guru to a large number of aspirants. I myself had the good fortune of being all along under his spiritual guidance, though not in a formal manner, for the last 15 years or so, though I knew him for over 43 years. Staying with him for a few days I came not only to revere him, but to love him as well, for his childlike simplicity, earnestness of purpose and the effective manner in which he conveyed great spiritual truths to us.

His house at Allahabad was a constant place of pilgrimage for high and low and I found scholars, High Court Judges and the elite of the Allahabad city visiting him with humility and reverence and obtaining guidance on many matters, secular and spiritual. Prof. Ranade combined in himself the best of the philosophies of the East as also of the West.

Prof. Ranade was a dynamic soul. It would always live in and work, wherever it is for the promotion of peace, as also for the spiritual welfare of mankind, as a whole. It will always guide earnest and even erring souls.”59

59-The Free Press Journal, June 20, 1957
