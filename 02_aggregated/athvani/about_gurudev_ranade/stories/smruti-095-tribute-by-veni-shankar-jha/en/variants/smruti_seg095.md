94- Padma Bhushan Dr. Veni Shankar Jha

Vice-Chancellor of Banaras Hindu University

“I heard his (Gurudev Ranade) lectures at Nagpur. Once he did me the honour of staying with me for a day at Nagpur. I recall his loving presence at home and how he radiated joy to everybody he came in contact with, by his very simple way of life.

I was sad when I heard that he was no more. I had the luxury of having spent a few quiet and joyful hours with him. The sense of void is still there; how very much the students of philosophy today need the inspiration and guidance of such an honest, independent and clear thinker as Prof. R. D. Ranade. Giants in the world of philosophy are rare!” 115

115- B.R.Kulkarni: The Glimpses of Shri Gurudev Ranade (1990) Pg. 22
