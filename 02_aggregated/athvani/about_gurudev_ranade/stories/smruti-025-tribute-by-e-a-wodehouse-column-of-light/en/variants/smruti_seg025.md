24- Prof. Ernest Armine Wodehouse

Principal, Deccan College, Poona

An English Theosophist, Professor of English at BHU

Prof. Wodehouse wrote to Ram (R. D. Ranade) as student- “Remember that the great object you should put before yourself is the spiritual raising and purification of India; and that this necessitates, before anything else, the discipline and purification of yourself.

As the latter process goes on, the means of influencing others will come of their own accord. Don’t think for the present so much of influencing others. Work at your own character and try and develop your own intuition and everything else will follow in due course. I shall of course help you, to the best of my ability….”  (Extract from letter dated on Oct. 23, 1908)30

Once in 1908, Shri Gurudev, Prof. Wodehouse, Shri Baburao Bhadbhade, Govindrao Kelkar, Haribhau Tulpule, Shri Vishnupant Karandikar and Shri Vasudevrao Paranjpye were discussing in the bungalow of Prof. Wodehouse about Carlyle’s ‘Sartor Resartus.’ At that time, Gurudev once casually peeped out of the window in front of him which was towards the direction of the holy place of Alandi of the great saint Jnaneswara. Just then, he perceived a huge column of Light extending high up in the firmament! Suddenly he got up from the chair, and consequently and reflexively all others also got up from their chairs. All the others could also see the column of light. And all were looking at it for nearly two or three minutes. Later the light gradually became faint and disappeared. Prof. Wodehouse then expressed in emotion, “Today my spiritual life began!”31
