73- Prof. Dr. Mohammad Hafiz Syed

Professor of Urdu, Allahabad University

Prof. Syed wrote to Prof. R. D. Ranade-

“Your letter made a deep impression upon my mind. You are a Brahmin in the true sense of the word. You love wisdom more than wealth. I am proud of your friendship. May you be spared long to serve our motherland!”89

89- A letter wrote to Gurudev Ranade by Prof. Syed on October 6, 1927
