87- Shri R. A. Jahagirdar

Vice-Chancellor, Karnatak University, Dharwad

“I feel it a great privilege and honour to offer on behalf of the Karnatak University a hearty welcome to our distinguished guest of today, Dr. R. D. Ranade.

Dr. Ranade needs no introduction to the people of Karnatak. We are legitimately proud that Karnatak has produced an eminent man of the type of Dr. Ranade. His academic career was very bright throughout his college course. He has been a great philosopher and a great saint.” 108

108- Shri R. A. Jahagirdar’s speech delivered on 26 August 1950 at Karnatak University, Dharwad
