45- Shri Mahadevbhai Desai

Personal Secretary of Mahatma Gandhi and Freedom Fighter

On 6th February 1921, Shri Mahadevbhai Desai wrote letter to Shri Gurudev Ranade expressing his gratitute towards their common friend the late Shri Bhaurao Yadwadkar Patwardhan. In the said letter he also remembered Shri Gurudev for his friendly and kind nature.

“Acquaintance with you, I take as a silver lining to the cloud that descended on me last December in Nagpur. He (Shri Bhaurao Yadwadkar Patwardhan) held you in reverence, he was anxious, whilst he was with me at Sinhgadh last June, to secure me the privilege of your acquaintance. But it was only to be through his death- I don’t say after his death. For his death binds me closer to you than even before. I count on your kindness and love whilst I say this. We must meet someday I know; I must benefit by your company someday I know, for God has blessed me with nothing so much as a full-more than a full-measure of that loving warmth that comes from dear friends. But I shall not tire you by making this letter any longer.”57

57- A Letter wrote to Gurudev Ranade by Mahadev Desai on 6th February 1921.
