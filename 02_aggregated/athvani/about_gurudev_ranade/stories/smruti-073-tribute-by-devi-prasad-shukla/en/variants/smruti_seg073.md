72- Pandit Devi Prasad Shukla

Principal, Malaviya College, Allahabad

Once Pandit Devi Prasad Shukla, was asked by his friends, ‘How is it Dr. Ranade has not written his autobiography?’ Pandit Shukla immediately replied, ‘Who says no? Here it is,’ and he at once put before them ‘The Pathway to God in Hindi Literature’ written by Gurudev Ranade.88

On 28-1-1951, in reference to Parmartha Sopan (Source book of The Pathway to God in Hindi Literature) Shri Gurudev mentioned to Pandit Devi Prasad Shukla, “There is nothing in this book, which I have not experienced.”
