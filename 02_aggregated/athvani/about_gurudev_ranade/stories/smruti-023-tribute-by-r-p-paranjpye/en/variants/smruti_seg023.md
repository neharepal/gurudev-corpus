22- Wrangler Sir R. P. Paranjpye

Professor of Mathematics and Principal, Fergusson College

India’s High Commissioner to Australia

Sir R. P. Paranjpye                                              Lord Chelmsford

“Ranade is a God intoxicated personality!”27

While Prof. Ranade was a professor at the Fergusson College, the then Viceroy Lord Chelmsford, was once a distinguished guest. Wrangler R. P. Paranjpye, the then Principal, received him at the gate. By the side of the Wrangler were four professors, one of Sanskrit, another of English, the third of Philosophy and the fourth, Prof. Ranade. The Wrangler, while introducing himself and others to the distinguished guest, is reported to have said, “Well, you see in each of the four a professor of this and that subject; but in Prof. Ranade, you can see all four rolled into one.”28
