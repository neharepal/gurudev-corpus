14- Smt. Shivalingavvakka

Senior devotee of Shri Bhausaheb Maharaj

In April 1925, Shri Gurudev built the first house at Nimbal (Now seen as Samadhi Mandir) and invited all devotees for the opening ceremony and celebrated three days spiritual saptah. Prominent devotees like Shri Amburao Maharaj, Shri Shettappa, Shri Girimalappa Maharaj, Shri Pundappa, Shri Nandeppa, Smt. Shivalingavvakka, Shri Bhimrao (son of Shri Bhausaheb Maharaj), Shri Kannur Swamiji and Shri Narasappa Shapeti (direct and last disciple of Shri Nimbargi Maharaj), attended the saptah. Shri Shettappa had brought the Holy ashes of Shri Bhausaheb Maharaj which were installed with devotion in the central front niche of the U-shaped hall. Smt. Shivalingavvakka wrote the following poetic lines when she went upstairs and stood on the parapet of the new building and looked at the spiritual vision of God: ‘साधुसंत चरणदर्शन नित्य व्हावें | साधु संतांच्या घरास | अनुभवाचा आरास || जाई जुईचा सुवास | आनंदाचा हव्यास ||’ (‘In this abode of the saint, There is the vision of Saints and Sages, And the splendour of experience, And the fragrance of Jasmine, And the shower of blissfulness.’) 17
