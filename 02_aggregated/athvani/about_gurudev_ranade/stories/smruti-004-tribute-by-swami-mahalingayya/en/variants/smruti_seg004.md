3- Swami Mahalingayya

The Spiritual Teacher of Gurudev’s Mother

When Gurudev (then Ramu) was an infant boy of five, mother Parvatibai had taken her child to, Swami Mahalingayya, her spiritual master, for his blessings. Swamiji used to sit in the verandah of Nandikeshwar temple in Jamkhandi.

The Swamiji looked at the child lying prostrate at his feet and then began to look at the sky with fixed and steady gaze. Swamiji remarked, 'Your son is a great saint of the future; so I must bow myself before him' and bowed himself before him. The mother Parvatibai was simply amazed with a sense of gratification and devotion.

Immediately after her arrival at home, she gave the child good bath, applied kumkum to his forehead, placed a garland of flowers round his neck and washed his pretty feet. The child Gurudev was astonished at this strange behaviour of his mother and asked her, “What is this mother?” The mother replied with a heavy heart and a choked throat, “My child! You are divinity in flesh and blood!”

Gurudev narrated this incident with full of emotions of gratefulness and unfathomable love to his mother and said, “I was looking at the man who was looking at the sky.”3
