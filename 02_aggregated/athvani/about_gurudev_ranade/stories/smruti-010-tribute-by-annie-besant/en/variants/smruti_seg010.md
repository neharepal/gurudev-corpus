9- Dr. Annie Besant

A British Socialist and President of the Theosophical Society

“Mr. Ranade, you have enriched our language by Coining new Phraseology.”9

Prof. Wodehouse affectionately took Mr. Ranade to meet Dr. Annie Besant, the then president of the Theosophical Society of India. When they met in Banaras in October 1908, Dr. Besant was greatly impressed by Mr. Ranade. She carefully heard all the details of his spiritual experiences and was astonished. She asked Rambhau whether he had been initiated by a master. Fortunately, Rambhau had taken a photograph of Shri Maharaj with him. He readily handed it over to her. She cast a penetrating glance at it and returned it to him with these remarks, “You are blessed by a respected Master. You have had great spiritual experiences. It’s a sign of pure morality. You are in safe hands.” Gurudev was elated by her comments.10
