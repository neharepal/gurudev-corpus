21- Shri M. R. Jayakar

First Vice-Chancellor, University of Poona

An Educationist and Philanthropist

Shri M. R. Jayakar while requesting Prof. Ranade to give a review on the Vedanta Philosophy, a book published by him and written by his maternal grandfather Shri Kirtikar, said:

“I know your scholarship and research in the field of Vedanta Philosophy, and I shall feel very grateful if you will kindly send me a review of this book after a careful perusal thereof, which I will get the ‘Bombay Chronicle’ to print in full.”26

26- A Letter wrote to Gurudev Ranade by M. R. Jayakar on 29 August 1924
