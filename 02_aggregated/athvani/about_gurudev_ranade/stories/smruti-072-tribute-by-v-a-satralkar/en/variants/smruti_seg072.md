71- Shri V. A. Satralkar

Member of Evangelistic Committee of Presbyterian Church in USA

“I cannot stop without writing a few words that incomparable pair, Prof. Bhate and Prof. Ranade. What a pleasure it was to sit at their feet and learn! Prof. Bhate’s mind was synthetic, that of Prof. Ranade was analytic. They both were complementary. The one was instructive as the other with his vast, up-to-date world of information, with his beetling brow, his dreamy eyes fixed on vacancy, and his sage-like face illumined with a rare smile.”87

87- History of the D.E.Society (1935) Part 2, Pg. 186
