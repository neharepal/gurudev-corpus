91- Dr. P. D. Khanolkar

A Prominent Physician (MD) and Civil Surgeon of Solapur

Dr. Khanolkar wrote a Health Bulletin of Gurudev R. D. Ranade-

“…..During the last three days before his departure (Niryan) he was completely detached from the world. He particularly got himself thoroughly examined, twelve hours before he left his body, by Dr. N. S. Paripatyadar of Solapur, as if, it seemed, to get confirmed that he was completely free from any disease. During these last five days he did not take even a morsel of food or a drop of water, but took particular care to get his bowels cleared by laxatives. The Doctor on examination found the lungs clear and he did not find any abnormality except extreme asthenia and it was astonishing for him to see that no faecal matter was lodged in the colon.

…Thus did the revered and elevated Soul depart, with a complete control over his disease, giving up his clear body to the Creator, as did Kabir and Shri Nimbargi Maharaj. The revered Soul passed away in joyous enjoyment of God, deeply engrossed in meditation and completely away from the material world.

Even after death (June 6, 1957, 10:32 PM), his body remained perfectly lustrous without any sign of rigor mortis or postmortem lividity for the next 18 hours till the final holy rites. I noticed with astonishment that the tip of his nose was particularly shining, and his iris was partially devoid of pigment and conjunctive, lustrous, whitish in colour.”112

112- M. S. Deshpande: Gurudev Ranade’s Life of Light (1963) Pg. 192
