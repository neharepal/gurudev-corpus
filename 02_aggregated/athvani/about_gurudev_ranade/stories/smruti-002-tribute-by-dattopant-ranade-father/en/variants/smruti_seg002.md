1- Shri Dattopant Ranade

The Father of Gurudev Ranade

“Without entertaining any feeling of ego and consciousness of pride, about the splendid success you have achieved at the matriculation examination, you should study hard with assiduity and firmness. ….. Hereafter all my responsibility rests with you. The Almighty God is, no doubt, merciful and kind enough to protect all of us.”1

(A Letter written to young Rambhau Ranade by his father on January 10, 1903)

S. N. Deshpande: Biography and Mysticism of Gurudev Ranade (1981) Pg. 34
