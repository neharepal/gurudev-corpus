31- Shripad Mahadev Mate

A Marathi Writer and Social Reformer

“The late Justice (Mahadev Govind) Ranade was rightly called the “prince” of graduates. A few years after his death, there came another Ranade, who has reached the highest peak of intellectual brilliance amongst our generation of all those who are around about 70. Spirituality is no doubt a valuable heritage of Indian culture but it remained neglected by our society. Prof. Ranade in his prime youth has offered a rational basis and justification to spirituality and devotion to God and has regained for it the original dignified position and honour in our society.”38

“A man who has received modern education develops a critical attitude on account of his reading, reflection and observation……., but this in itself is not sufficient to give an account of the way the mystical life progresses……. Among the learned men of today, I think, Prof. Rambhau Ranade is the only person who is conversant with modern ideas and who has traversed the mystical path himself.”39

38- S.N. Deshpande: Biography &amp; Mysticism of Gurudev Ranade (1981) Pg. xxxiii
