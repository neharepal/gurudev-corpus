98- Dr. Surendra S. Barlingay

Chairman, Maharashtra State Board for Literature and Culture, Bombay

“It is our great privilege to publish ‘Studies in Indian Philosophy’, by late Prof. R. D. Ranade, a doyen of Indian Philosophy.

The Advaita system itself, to which Shri Ranade belongs, has developed on account of the intellectual warfare with other philosophical systems and the conflicts arising within the system itself. Comparisons and criticisms have always helped development of philosophical thought. This is borne out by the present work also, though briefly. We wish we had a fuller exposition of such comparison from this great master.

What more befitting tribute can be paid to late Prof. R. D. Ranade, than bringing to light his hitherto unpublished works?” 119
