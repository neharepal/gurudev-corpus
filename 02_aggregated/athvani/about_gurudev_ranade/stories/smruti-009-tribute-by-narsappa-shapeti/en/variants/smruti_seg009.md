8- Shri Narsappa Shapeti

An Ardent disciple of Shri Nimbargi Maharaj

He was called as ‘कडेहुट्ट मगा’ (The Last Disciple/ Son). He used to visit Nimbal and meet Prof. Ranade while going to his home. Once while he was at Nimbal he said to Prof. Ranade, “How wonderful is this house which you have built here, in any part of it is heard the unstruck sound (अनाहतनाद) of the name of God.”8
