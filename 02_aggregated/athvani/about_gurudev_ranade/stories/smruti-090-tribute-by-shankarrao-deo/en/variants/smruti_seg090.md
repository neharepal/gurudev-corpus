89- Shri Shankarrao DeoGeneral Secretary of the Congress

Member of Constituent Assembly of India

“Even though my ears were hearing his (Gurudev R. D. Ranade) lecture, my eyes were looking at him with a fixed and steady gaze. And I was enjoying the sight of his face more than hearing his lecture. Because the vision of his face immediately convinces anybody that he is a realised soul. If we have to look at the facial expression of these exalted saints - especially their eyes - we would easily realise that these persons, though moving on this earth, are really living in quite a different world.”110                                                                                                         (Remarked when he attended Gurudev Ranade's lecture at Rashtrapati Bhavan, New Delhi in 1954)

110- S. N. Deshpande: Biography &amp; Mysticism of Gurudev Ranade (1981) Pg. xviii
