61- Padma Bhushan Sir C. A. Patwardhan

The Rajasaheb of Sangli

“In Gurudev Ranade Saheb we can find the rarest mingling of a great world-renowned scholar of philosophy and a masterly mystic who stands a beacon light for the suffering humanity. This unique and rare combination of an exceptional genius and a realized mystic is aptly described in our scriptures as “शाब्दे परे च निष्णातम्.”75

“The noblest, the grandest and the most glorious that you can conceive of may be done. You are the architect, we the humble tools in your hands.” (a letter dated June 29, 1947) “Today, Ashadha Suddha Ekadashi, the foundation Day.”          (Replied to Rajasaheb by Gurudev on the same day with signature and established       ‘The Adhyatma Vidhya Mandir, Sangli.’)76

“गुरुवर्य रानडे साहेब हे केवळ तत्वज्ञानाचे प्रोफेसर नसून ते आत्मसाक्षात्कारी संत होते. परमार्थ आणि भक्ति हा त्यांच्या जीवनाचा स्थायी-भाव होता. आपला एक क्षणहि वाया न घालवितां त्यांनीं परमार्थ आचरिला. नैतिक आचरण विशुद्ध ठेवून, अधिकारी सत्पुरुषाकडून मिळालेल्या नामाचें अखंड स्मरण केल्यास गुरुकृपेच्या बळावर परमेश्वराचा साक्षात्कार ‘याचि देहीं याचि डोळां’ होणें शक्य आहे, असें त्यांचें सांगणें असे.”77
