27- Prof. H. G. Raulinson

Principal, Deccan College, Poona

An Oxford trained Historian, Professor of English

Prof. Raulinson wrote a letter to Mr. R. D. Ranade in 1906-

“Ranade, your letter is received. Your love for Indian Freedom is natural. Your attraction towards current Indian Freedom is also natural. But you are not born for India’s political advancement but for India’s philosophical advancement and I feel that you should try your best in this field.”34
