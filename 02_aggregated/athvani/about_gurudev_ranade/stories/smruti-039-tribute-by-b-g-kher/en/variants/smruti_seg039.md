38- Padma Vibhushan Shri B. G. Kher

Chief Minister of Bombay State and Gujarat State

“I shared with Prof. Ranade the Sanskrit prize at the University examination. What a great good fortune would it be if I were to share his spiritual prize as well!”50

(ज्याप्रमाणे जीवनांत रामभाऊंना व मला ही शिष्यवृत्ती विभागून मिळाली त्याप्रमाणे गुरुदेवांच्या आध्यात्मिक बलाचा थोडा जरी कृपांश मला त्यांनी वाटून दिला तरी मी स्वतःला धन्य समजेन!)

50- Shri B. G. Kher’s speech delivered on 6 April 1947 at Poona. (Koushik Endowment Lectures by Shri Gurudev)
