57- Padma Bhushan Dr. D. C. Pavate

Vice-Chancellor, Karnataka University, Dharwad

Governor of Punjab

“Dr. Ranade was a great scholar of international repute. Dr. Ranade himself was one of the great mystics of modern times and, grounded as he was in deep mystic experience, he has expounded mystical philosophy as few have been able to do. Among his great works are included ‘Mysticism in Maharashtra’ and ‘Pathway to God in Hindi Literature.’ With such an eminent scholar and philosopher in our midst, we of the Karnatak University could hardly resist the temptation of requesting Dr. Ranade to write a book on Mysticism in Kannada literature, on the lines he had already done on mysticism in Marathi and Hindi literatures. So, the University invited him to deliver a course of twenty lectures on Mysticism in Karnataka, which could be later on published by the University in the form of a book. To that, he readily agreed…..

He followed a novel and very interesting method of delivering his lectures.”71

71- R. D. Ranade: Pathway to God in Kannada Literature  (1960)  Pg. vii
