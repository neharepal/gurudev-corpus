34- Shri K. M. Munshi

Founder of Bharatiya Vidhya Bhavan, Bombay

Governor of Uttar Pradesh

“Gurudev Ranade was born in Jamkhandi in Karnataka. As he said, spiritual life had been the beginning of his philosophical career. He taught philosophy at the Fergusson College, Poona and was later Head of the Department of Philosophy in the Allahabad University. He retired as Vice-Chancellor of that University in 1946. A profound thinker and devotee whose heart was always set on God, Gurudev Ranade combined in himself deep philosophical insight with heights of mystical experience.”42
