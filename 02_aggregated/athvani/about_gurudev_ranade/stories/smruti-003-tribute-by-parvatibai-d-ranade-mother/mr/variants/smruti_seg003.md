The Mother of Gurudev Ranade

In 1909, Rambhau suffered terribly from TB of the brain. His mother and his young wife were extremely anxious about his health. It was only the unseen power of meditation on the spiritual Name by the pious mother and the devout son, that was the sure source of relief. His mother used to wake him up for morning meditation in spite of his illness. She said, “रामू ऊठ, जेवढा होईल तेवढा नेम कर, मग पुनः वाटले तर नीज, पण ही वेळ घालवूं नको” “Oh my child Ramu, get up, meditate on Divine Name, as this is the most precious time in the early hours of morning.”    (These exact words of his mother were reported by Gurudev while narrating about his deadly disease.)2

2- S. N. Deshpande: Biography and Mysticism of Gurudev Ranade (1981) Pg. 65
